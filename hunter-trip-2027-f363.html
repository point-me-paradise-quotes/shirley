Step Two
Your Resort
Value Resort · Walt Disney World
Disney's All-Star Resorts
Sports, Music, or Movies — Preferred Room
Animal Kingdom Resort Area, Walt Disney World, Orlando FL
The All-Star Resorts sit at the southern end of Disney property, about a five-minute bus ride from Animal Kingdom and fifteen from Magic Kingdom. What you get here is exactly what makes the difference for a trip like this: a fully immersive Disney environment without the price tag of the deluxe tier. The buildings are color-saturated and outsized — at All-Star Music you walk past 40-foot saxophones; at Movies, giant Dalmatians flank the stairwells; at Sports, massive football helmets anchor the courtyards. None of it is subtle, and that's the point.

The Preferred room you're booked into is in the section closest to Melody Hall (or its equivalent at whichever All-Star Disney assigns you) — that means the shortest walk to the food court, the bus stop, the main pool, and check-in. On a week in July, that proximity matters. The standard rooms run 260 square feet with a fold-down Murphy-style second bed that doubles as a table and two chairs when not deployed — genuinely useful engineering in a small space. The main bed is a queen; the fold-down is also queen-width. Four people fit comfortably, and the bathroom is split into three zones (sink, toilet, shower) so mornings don't become a bottleneck.

What's Included in Your Package

  • 6 Nights — Preferred Room, All-Star Resort (Sports, Music, or Movies). Views of courtyard, pool, or parking area. Close to main lobby, food court, and bus stop.
  • 7-Day Theme Park Tickets — Park Hopper for all 4 travelers. Valid for entry to Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, and Animal Kingdom. Park Hopper allows you to visit more than one park on the same day — start at one, hop to another after noon. 7 days of tickets gives you flexibility to take rest days or return to favorites without losing access.
  • Disney Dining Plan — per person, per night of stay:
    • 1 Table Service meal (sit-down restaurant dining)
    • 1 Quick Service meal (counter-service: burgers, pizza, sandwiches, etc.)
    • 1 Snack or non-alcoholic drink — or 1 alcoholic drink for guests 21+ (beer, wine, cocktail); snack options include ice cream, popcorn, piece of fruit, cup of soup
    • 1 Resort-Refillable Drink Mug — unlimited soft drink refills at your resort food court only (not valid in theme parks)
  • Memory Maker ($185 value) — Disney PhotoPass's all-inclusive photo package. Every photo and video captured by Disney photographers at attractions, character meet-and-greets, and around the parks is downloadable for your group. No paying per photo.
  • Travel Protection Plan ($297) — trip protection coverage for the package.
  • 4 Miniature Golf Vouchers — one round of 18-hole mini golf per person.
  • 4 ESPN Wide World of Sports Vouchers — general admission for one day per guest to the ESPN Wide World of Sports Complex.
  • Package Discounts — various resort, dining, and recreation discounts included.
  • Free Bus Transportation — service to all four theme parks, both water parks, and Disney Springs. Buses typically run every 20 minutes from the resort stop.
  • Early Theme Park Entry — as an on-site Disney resort guest, your party enters any theme park 30 minutes before general public opening.
  • MagicBand / App Room Key Access — use the My Disney Experience app on your phone as your room key, park ticket, and charge card.

Not Included — Good to Know

  • Lightning Lane passes (formerly Genie+) — purchased separately in the My Disney Experience app, starting at $15–$30/person/day depending on season. Popular for skipping standby lines on rides like Tron, Guardians, and Frozen Ever After.
  • Airport transfers — quoted separately below once flights are confirmed.
  • Alcoholic beverages beyond the included dining plan snack/drink credit.
  • Gratuities at table-service restaurants (standard 18–20%).
  • Souvenirs, Mickey bars, and any retail purchases.

Pools
Two pools on property. The Calypso Pool is guitar-shaped with a Donald Duck fountain and an adjacent kiddie wading pool. The Piano Pool is a secondary option, also large. Pool bar on-site. Movies Under the Stars (Sun–Fri nights) at the main pool.
Dining & Shopping
Intermission Food Court (All-Star Music) offers multiple stations: specialty coffee, roast chicken, burgers, pizza, pasta, grab-and-go. Singing Spirits Pool Bar. Maestro Mickey's gift shop with Disney merchandise and resort essentials.
Recreation
Note'able Games Arcade, playground (near Jazz Inn section), jogging trail, campfire activities at the Calypso Pool. Laundry facilities on-site.
Transport
Free bus service to Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom, Typhoon Lagoon, Blizzard Beach, and Disney Springs. Runs approximately every 20 minutes. Bus stop at Melody Hall.
